🇪🇸 Menorca Resorts

Why Menorca? UNESCO Biosphere Reserve with the best beaches in the Balearics — pristine turquoise coves, fewer crowds, and the 185km Camí de Cavalls coastal hiking trail. Quieter and more romantic than Mallorca, with excellent local seafood dining. Trade-offs: fewer luxury hotel options at the €800+ tier, less dramatic mountain scenery, smaller dining/nightlife scene.

Getting there: JFK → Madrid/Barcelona → Mahón (\~same as Mallorca). Or from Mallorca: 45-min flight or 1.5-hr fast ferry.

🇪🇸 Ibiza Resorts

Why Ibiza? Beyond the famous club scene, Ibiza has a stunning natural side — UNESCO World Heritage Old Town (Dalt Vila), beautiful north-coast coves, bohemian-chic villages, outstanding dining, and a wellness/yoga culture. The north of the island is peaceful, pine-forested, and feels worlds away from the party strip. Sunsets are legendary.

The honest trade-off vs. Mallorca: Ibiza's luxury hotel scene at the €800–1,200 tier is thinner than Mallorca's — fewer "fortress-level" unique properties. The south/west party reputation is real (Playa d'en Bossa, San Antonio), but the north coast is serene. Dining scene is excellent and cosmopolitan. Beaches are beautiful but generally smaller coves.

Getting there: JFK → Madrid/Barcelona → Ibiza (\~same connections as Mallorca). Inter-island: Mallorca → Ibiza is a 30-min flight or 2-hr fast ferry.
