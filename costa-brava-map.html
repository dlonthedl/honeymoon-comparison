🚗 Costa Brava Honeymoon Road Trip

July 23–30, 2026 — Barcelona → Tossa de Mar → Baix Empordà → Girona

🗺️ Driving Route

Day 2-3: Barcelona (2 nights) — arrive, explore the city

Day 4-5: Drive to Tossa de Mar (1 hr) — beaches, medieval walls, coves

Day 6-7: Drive north to Mas de Torrent (1 hr) — villages, coves, spa, food

Day 8: Drive to Girona (30 min) — cathedral, Jewish Quarter, final dinner

Day 9: Girona → Barcelona airport (1 hr) — fly home

🏨 Elisabeth by the Sea — Tossa de Mar

🏨 Mas de Torrent — Baix Empordà
